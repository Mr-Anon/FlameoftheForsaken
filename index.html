Book Overview

In a kingdom far beyond, the breath of betrayal sweeps its way on the royal bloodline. Abandoned and banished, the sole survivor of the backstabbing must find vengeance against the old Vizier who usurped the throne.

Resigned to a fate of slavery by her sister, Felisin must exact vengeance on her own kin and fight for justice. In another city, Moravid longs to wreak havoc on the murderers of his family.

In this sizzling tale of vengeance, sprinkled with extensive use of algorithms, watch multiple tales of revenge intertwine and fold into one as an old enemy awakens.
